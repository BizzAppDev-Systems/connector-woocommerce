This module's primary functionality is to enable the import of customers from WooCommerce into Odoo.
To import customers from WooCommerce, add the required credentials to the WooCommerce backend, and then select the 'Import Partners in Background' button.

When you activate the 'Allow Partners without Email' boolean option in the WooCommerce backend, it permits the import of partners even if they do not have an associated email address.

Customers Present in WooCommerce.

Customers imported into Odoo from WooCommerce along with their respective child partners.
