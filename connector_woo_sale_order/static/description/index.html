This module's primary functionality is to enable the import of Customers,Products,Categories,Attributes,Attribute Values, and Sale Order from WooCommerce into Odoo and Export Sale Order Status form Odoo to WooCommerce.
To Import Customers,Products,Categories,Attributes, Attribute Values and Sale Order from WooCommerce, add the required credentials to the WooCommerce backend, and then select the 'Import Partners' button to import Customers, 'Import Product Attributes' button to import Attributes and its Values,select the 'Import Product Categories' button to import Categories, select the 'Import Products' button to import Products and select the 'Import Sale Orders' button to import Orders from WooCommerce.

When you activate the 'Allow Partners without Email' and 'Allow Partners without SKU' boolean options in the WooCommerce backend, they permit the import of partners without associated email addresses and products without SKUs, respectively, while during product import into Odoo, you can select a 'Product Category,' and enabling the 'Mark Order Completed On Delivery' option exports Sale Orders to 'Completed' status, and when combined with 'Send Tracking Information,' it also sends the 'Tracking Reference' at the Delivery Order level to WooCommerce.

Warning for 'The Price Tax and Total Tax do not match' Due to Tax Configuration Issues:This warning is triggered when there is a discrepancy between the calculated price tax and the total tax amount, typically due to inconsistencies in tax settings or applied tax rates.

Exporting Sale Order to 'Completed' State in WooCommerce:After validating the Delivery Order, and it transitions to the 'Done' state, a 'Export Delivery Status' button becomes visible at the Sale Order level, allowing you to export the Sale Order to the 'Completed' state in WooCommerce.




Sale Order Status in WooCommerce:The Sale Order is in the 'Completed' status in WooCommerce.

Enabling 'Mark Order Completed On Delivery' with 'Send Tracking Information':When you enable both 'Mark Order Completed On Delivery' and 'Send Tracking Information,' the Sale Order will be sent to the 'Completed' status in WooCommerce, and it will also send the 'Tracking Reference' at the Delivery Order level to WooCommerce.

Imported Sale Order With Pending status in Odoo.

Sale Order with Status 'Pending' in WooCommerce.

Exporting 'Tracking Reference' and Changing Status to 'Complete' in WooCommerce:To Export the 'Tracking Reference' at the Delivery Order level and transition it to the 'Done' state, you can click on the 'Export Delivery Status' button at the Sale Order level in WooCommerce, effectively updating the status and exporting the 'Tracking Reference' to the 'Completed' state



Exporting Status to 'Complete' and Tracking Reference in WooCommerce:This action results in the status of the Sale Order being updated to 'Complete' and the corresponding Tracking Reference being exported within WooCommerce.
