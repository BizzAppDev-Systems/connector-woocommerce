Key Features of the Woocommerce Connector
  • Import Partners.
  • Import Products, Product Categories, Product Attributes and Product Tags.
  • Update Stock Inventory.
  • Import Taxes.
  • Import Sale orders.
  • Exporting Sale order status and Tracking Information to Woocommerce.

Backend Views
Woocommerce backend with required credentials and import functionality.

Woocommerce backend Advanced Configuration tab.

Partner Management
Customers present in Woocommerce.

Imported Partner view.

Product Management
Product present in Woocommerce.

Imported Product view.

Product categories present in Woocommerce.

Access Woocommerce categories by clicking the "Woocommerce Product Categories" in the backend menu.

Product Attributes with Values present in Woocommerce.

Imported Product Attribute and additionally, you can retrieve updated information of attribute values by clicking
"IMPORT ATTRIBUTE VALUE".

Product tags present in Woocommerce.

Imported Woocommerce Product Tags view.

Inventory Management
Set the stock quantity for the product.

Clicking the "UPDATE STOCK INVENTORY" button at the product form view level allows for exporting the updated stock quantity of a
particular product to WooCommerce. Additionally, at the backend level, the same button can be utilized to update stock quantities
in batch.

Tax Management
Tax present in Woocommerce.

Access Woocommerce Taxes by clicking the "WooCommerce Taxes" in the backend menu.

Sale Order Management
Orders present in Woocommerce.

Imported Sale Order view.

In Woocommerce backend, when you enable "Mark Order Completed On Delivery" the "Send Tracking Information" will be
visible.If both the boolean are True, the Sale Order will be set to the 'Completed' status in Woocommerce, and it will also
send the 'Tracking Information' to the Woocommerce.

After validating the delivery order, the "EXPORT DELIVERY STATUS" button will be visible in the sale order. Clicking this button will
update the WooCommerce order status to 'Completed' in both Woocommerce and the Odoo sale order.
